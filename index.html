hello there!

Hello! Thank you for stopping by! I've been a lover of books for as long as I can remember, but really got into writing reviews and sharing my opinions on what I read just a few years ago. For some reason, I used to be a bit shy about sharing this information (probably because a lot of my reads fall into the 'smut' category). There was a book I read back in 2019 (the one I'm holding here in the picture) that really took me out of my shell. It reconnected me with all the things I loved about reading. I really just felt like sharing it with whoever would listen, which ultimately resulted in my bookstagram page. Through the bookstagram community, I was able to connect with fellow readers and authors who shared the same passion as I did about all things books. I'm not sure where this webpage will go, but I hope to anchor myself into the book-ish community! Looking forward to sharing my favorite books, quotes and upcoming reads! For the most up to date information, follow my bookstagram account and follow me/friend me on Goodreads! (linked above). Happy Reading!
